Why Should I Care About Service-Learning?

Service-Learning integrates life experiences with academia to promote educational success and personal growth by granting students the opportunity to connect with their local community and, potentially, beyond. By developing a deeper connection with local communities, students can further their education by understanding the purpose and application of knowledge gained from the classroom to the real world. Watch our video to learn more about our team and Service-Learning!

Student Benefits to Service-Learning

  • Provides dynamic experiences in practical environments and situations.
  • Enriches a reflective understanding of the course content by helping others.
  • Networks opportunities by involving your interests towards the community.
  • Increases social development by enhancing mental health and change.
  • Brings career guidance and preparation for competitive job markets
  • Little to no impact on coursework grades and GPA for ANY major.
